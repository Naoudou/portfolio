Autonomous Seed sorting system

This project introduces an automated seed sorting machine powered by Artificial Intelligence (CNN) and the Internet of Things (IoT). Using a Raspberry Pi, camera, and servo motor, the system captures images of seeds (peanuts, white beans, corn), classifies them as "good" or "bad" using a CNN model, and sorts them automatically. A web application (ASP.NET) allows for remote monitoring and control. The results show high accuracy, reduced human error and sorting time, and the possibility to adapt to different seed types, though improvements are needed for larger seeds and environmental conditions.
